About me

I am driven by the conviction that data empowers better decision-making. My goal is to simplify code comprehension and facilitate efficient data utilization for individuals. I advocate for open data and open source software. In the realm of data analysis, I predominantly leverage Excel, MySQL, Tableau, and Python. For web development, my toolkit includes HTML, CSS, SCSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, React.js, and Python.

I have professional experience as a Field Maintenance Engineer at Huawei Technologies Company, where I acquired hands-on expertise in the management and troubleshooting of cell tower sites. My educational background includes graduation from Sule Lamido University Kafin Hausa, where I delved into diverse areas such as software engineering, networking, data science, data visualization, and user research.
