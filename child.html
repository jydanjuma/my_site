Child Daycare Enrollment System

ABSTRACT

In our society, initiating a new change or process is quite a difficult task. Although the use of computers and its application has taken off effectively in Nigeria, its diversified use in all sectors of the economy still remains in tall order. Gold, Rachel. (2018). Children’s Daycare centres have found computerization of their activities very useful in record keeping, information tracking and updating as well as decision making. Today, the case for accuracy has resulted to the shift from the use of manual approaches in handling Children’s Daycare activities to computerized approach. Currently, computers are made to handle these tasks more efficiently than the manual system of recording information that has existed. Introducing the computer program called Children’s Daycare Management system will assist Daycare centres to automate their company online and parents can register their children’s to a daycare centre via online respectively. The computer based program will present this information on the computer screen for use by the daycare centres and the parent computer screen. To ensure a standardized object oriented program in its entire ramification, HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT, PHP and MYSQL Database was used in the development of Children Daycare Management System. These entire programs are used to ensure effective program. The motive behind the use of the language is its compatibility with several Operating Systems. It is object oriented and combines the feature of hypertext Preprocessor (PHP) and JavaScript platform thereby making it to run on any Operating System.
